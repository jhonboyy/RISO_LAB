Welcome to John Boy Riso Lab! With this app, you can submit your own pictures, transform the RGB channels into Riso colors and download them for printing:

1. Upload your photo.
2. Choose from over 70 colors for the main channels.
3. Select a pattern for the Riso texture.
4. Play around with and modify the values of the textures.
5. Once you're satisfied, click "Download result" to download your model, conveniently divided into layers.

Please note that this version is still a work in progress, and some functionalities may not be operational.
